Enriched expo experience


Event coordinators should provide the opportunity for exhibitors to capture attendee’s attention in these often distracting environments. Our smartphone app is easy to incorporate, easy-to-use and allows exhibitors to upload content, import product information and provide special offers for event attendees. Displaying relevant content is the easiest way to both attract the attention of a consumer and help businesses stand out from the competition. Advantages of beacon-enabled events:

  • Exhibitors can often miss out on potential business while trying to connect with each visitor at their booth. iBeacons can relay important information directly to potential customer’s smartphones and peak their interest. 
  • Exhibitors have complete control over content. They can easily update the app to alert customers of changes or updates in event information. 
  • Event coordinators can send ad hoc alerts to notify visitors of special events and information right as they enter the event.
  • Each phone application incorporates the ExpoFlyer app that allow exhibitors to upload content, import product information and provide special offers for event attendees. Visitors can easily swipe their phone screen when receiving a notification; they don’t have to actively have the application running on their phone.
  • Attendees can evaluate the event by providing feedback via their smartphone. Event coordinators can trigger these events at the end of each venue or when a visitor leaves the event.

How do I get started?

The set up is straightforward and non technical. All you need are iBeacon devices and a mobile application. iBeacons are small and relatively low cost, Bluetooth low energy (BLE) devices. They transmit small packets of information, from distances up to 50 metres depending on the environment. When a smartphone comes into range, the beacon's signal triggers content from either a local database or cloud-based storage and the user will see a notification on their home screen. They are powered using a coin cell batteries and can run for up to two years. Our iBeacon device to simply fixes your stand or kiosk application using self adhesive tape or similar. The ExpoFlyer app allows exhibitors to upload content, import product information and provide special offers for event attendees.

How accurate is the indoor location system?

iBeacon is a proximity based solution and suitable for presence detection and simple guidance scenarios. iBeacon technology has received a global attention after appearing in iOS 7 and is a good alternative to Wi-Fi RTLS. Its an alternative to complex and expensive Wi-Fi location systems and can provide location resolution down sub 2 metres.

Do you have any statistics about increased conversion rates with this technology ?

Market research from global technology analyses state that from short term campaigns, during which time more than 74,000 devices were asked to download the content, of those reached with iBeacon, 7.2% opted in and 75% of downloaders engaged directly with the content and actively used the incentives and content.

What smartphones does this technology work with ?

iOS devices including the iPhone 4S, 5, 5C and 5S, the iPad 3 and later, iPad Mini, all support iBeacons. iBeacon is Apple's brand name for this technology, but iBeacons work fine with Android devices too! Many Android devices running Android 4.3 or later detect iBeacons, including the Samsung Galaxy S3/S4/S4 Mini, Samsung Galaxy Note 2/3, HTC One, Nexus 4, 5 and 7.
